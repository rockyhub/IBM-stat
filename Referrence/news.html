Recognize an IBMer today!

25/10/2016

IBMer Appreciation Cards are designed for IBMers to use to recognize colleagues who demonstrate our Purpose, Values and Practices. You are encouraged to give them as part of your daily work habit of showing thanks and appreciation. And, you can share your thanks and appreciation by copying recipients' managers at the same time you give them.

BlueThx enables you to share your recognition with a recipient's social network by displaying a BlueThx badge with the Practice they demonstrated and the reason they are being thanked on both your and your recipient's Connections profile. Further, others within the social network may then comment and congratulate your recipient, thereby strengthening IBMers' digital reputations.
